Decision Support System for Credit Applications in Cooperatives Using Simple Additive Weighting Method

Description

A Decision Support System for Credit Applications in Cooperatives refers to a computer-based system that assists in the decision-making process of granting credit to members of a cooperative. The system is designed to analyze and evaluate the creditworthiness of applicants based on various criteria such as their financial history, credit score, and other relevant factors

The system provides recommendations to the decision-makers in the cooperative, who can then use this information to make informed decisions about whether or not to grant credit to the applicant

The use of such a system can help cooperatives streamline their credit application process, reduce the risk of bad loans, and improve the overall efficiency of their operations
